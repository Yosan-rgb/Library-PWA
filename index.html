    INSTALL ON iPHONE

    Add to Home Screen

    You can add this app to your home screen so it works like a normal app.

    • 1

      Open this app's link in Safari (or any other browsers).

    • 2

      Tap the Share button at the bottom center of the screen.

    • 3

      Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.

    • 4

      Tap Add in the top right corner.

    • 5

      The app icon appears on your home screen. It will now work fully offline as well!

    PDF TO EPUB CONVERTER

    Converting a PDF to EPUB

    This works best with text-based PDFs.

    Go to the PDF → EPUB tab, pick your file, and tap Convert. The newly converted EPUB should show up in your library when it's done.

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    How do I change my name in the app?›

    Go to the Home tab. Clear this site's data in your iPhone Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data. When reopned, the app will prompt a new name entry. All your books can be reuploaded from Files

    Where are my books saved outside of the app?›

    Books are stored locally on your device. To save a copy outside the app, use the Download EPUB button after converting a PDF and find the book in your iPhone's Files app.

    How can I share books with my friends?›

    After converting a PDF, tap Download EPUB to save the file. You can then share it via AirDrop, iMessage, email... ect from your Files app. Friends can acess the book with the same app or any other!.
